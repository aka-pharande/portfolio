Who am I ?

Hello there! My name is Aakanksha, and I am an experienced architect who has transitioned into the exciting world of front-end development and UX design. With over 7 years of experience in architecture, I have a unique perspective and skill set that I bring to the field of technology. I have developed a keen eye for detail, a deep understanding of design principles, and the ability to think critically about complex problems.

What I love the most about web development is the ability to create something tangible and impactful at a rapid pace. With my expertise in both design and front-end development, I bring a fresh perspective and innovative solutions to projects, standing at the intersection of design and code.

Recently, I discovered cloud technologies and was fascinated by their potential for hosting websites. I am eager to explore this area further and discover how it can revolutionize web development.
